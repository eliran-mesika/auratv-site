Review flow: No first-party account is required; the built-in safe demo can be opened on first launch.

What the app helps with

  • Importing M3U playlist URLs, M3U files, and Xtream-compatible credentials from sources you control
  • Reading guide data such as EPG or XMLTV when your source provides it
  • Favorites, watch history, continue watching, and cross-device library continuity
  • Optional QR transfer between your own devices on the same local network

Troubleshooting

  • Source import fails: confirm the playlist URL, file, host, username, and password are valid and authorized.
  • Guide data is missing: make sure the source includes an EPG or XMLTV feed and that the guide URL is reachable.
  • Playback does not start: check the stream URL from your provider and make sure the device still has network access.
  • iCloud sync: verify iCloud is enabled on the device if you expect playlists or history to appear across your own Apple devices.

What to include in a support request

  • App version and device model
  • Whether the issue affects import, guide data, sync, or playback
  • A redacted example of the playlist format or provider setup steps, without sending private credentials publicly
