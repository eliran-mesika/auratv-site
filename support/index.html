Subscription model: Aura TV Premium is an annual auto-renewable subscription. Eligible Apple IDs may see a 7-day introductory trial when the App Store purchase sheet offers it.

What the app helps with

  • Importing M3U playlist URLs, M3U files, and Xtream-compatible credentials from sources you control
  • Reading guide data such as EPG or XMLTV when your source provides it
  • Favorites, watch history, continue watching, and cross-device library continuity
  • Optional QR transfer between your own devices on the same local network

Subscription and billing help

  • Restore Purchases: use the in-app restore action while signed in to the same Apple ID that originally started the subscription.
  • Trial and renewals: if the App Store shows a 7-day introductory trial for your Apple ID, the subscription renews annually unless canceled at least 24 hours before the renewal date shown by Apple.
  • Reinstall behavior: deleting and reinstalling the app on the same Apple ID does not create a new introductory offer. Trial eligibility is controlled by Apple for the subscription group.
  • Cancel subscription: manage cancellation from Apple account subscription settings on your device.
  • Refunds and billing: Apple handles payment processing and refund decisions. Aura TV does not collect your full payment card number.

Troubleshooting

  • Source import fails: confirm the playlist URL, file, host, username, and password are valid and authorized.
  • Guide data is missing: make sure the source includes an EPG or XMLTV feed and that the guide URL is reachable.
  • Playback does not start: check the stream URL from your provider and make sure the device still has network access.
  • iCloud sync: verify iCloud is enabled on the device if you expect playlists or history to appear across your own Apple devices.
  • Subscription plans do not load: confirm the device can reach Apple services, relaunch the app, and try restore again before contacting support.

What to include in a support request

  • App version and device model
  • Whether the issue affects import, guide data, sync, or playback
  • A redacted example of the playlist format or provider setup steps, without sending private credentials publicly
